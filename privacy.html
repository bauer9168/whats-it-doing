Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026

What's it Doing? is an automotive repair consultation service operated by Rodgers Services LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the website and paid diagnostic consult service, how that information is used, and how text/email communication is handled.

Information we collect

When you submit or use a consult, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number if provided.
  • Vehicle information such as year, make, model, mileage, symptoms, diagnostic trouble codes, repair history, and related notes.
  • Photos, audio notes, documents, estimates, or other files you choose to upload.
  • Payment status and transaction identifiers from our payment processor.
  • Messages and updates added to your consult thread.
  • Basic technical information needed to operate and secure the website, such as timestamps, browser/device information, and server logs.

How we use information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the paid automotive diagnostic consultation you requested.
  • Create, maintain, and secure your consult thread.
  • Send your receipt, paid-thread link, case status updates, and replies related to your consult.
  • Review submitted vehicle symptoms, photos, audio notes, files, and repair details.
  • Maintain business records, troubleshoot the service, prevent misuse, and comply with legal/accounting obligations.

SMS/text messaging program

If you provide your phone number and choose call/text updates during consult intake, you agree to receive transactional text messages from What's it Doing? / Rodgers Services LLC related to your automotive consult. Messages may include your paid consult thread link, case status updates, reply notifications, and related active-consult updates.

Message frequency varies based on consult activity. Message and data rates may apply. SMS consent is optional and is not required to purchase a consult. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or use your active consult thread.

No sale or marketing sharing of mobile/SMS information

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise provide your mobile phone number, SMS opt-in information, or messaging consent information to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Mobile phone numbers and messaging consent information are used only to provide transactional consult-related communications and to operate the messaging service.

Service providers

We may use service providers to operate the website, payment processing, email delivery, SMS delivery, file storage, hosting, database services, security, and business records. These providers are used only as needed to deliver and operate the consult service and are not authorized to use mobile/SMS consent information for their own marketing.

Payments

Payments are processed by a third-party payment processor. We do not store your full card number on our website or in our database.

Uploaded files and consult content

Photos, audio notes, and files submitted with your consult may be reviewed as part of the diagnostic process. Do not upload sensitive personal information that is not necessary for the vehicle consult.

Data retention

Consult information may be retained for business records, customer support, dispute prevention, service improvement, and legal/accounting purposes. You may request deletion of non-required personal information by contacting Rodgers Services LLC through the website or your consult thread.

Your choices

  • You may choose not to provide a phone number.
  • You may choose whether to consent to call/text consult updates.
  • You may opt out of SMS by replying STOP.
  • You may request help by replying HELP.
  • You may request correction or deletion of certain personal information, subject to business, legal, and accounting record requirements.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Rodgers Services LLC through the What's it Doing? website or your active consult thread.
