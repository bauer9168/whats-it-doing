Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

What's it Doing? is an automotive diagnostic consultation service operated by Rodgers Services LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we handle call, text, and email communication related to paid consults.

Information We Collect

When you submit a consult, we may collect the information you provide, including your name, email address, phone number, vehicle information, mileage, symptoms, diagnostic codes, repair history, photos, audio notes, uploaded files, payment status, and messages sent through the consult thread.

How We Use Information

We use your information to review your automotive concern, create and maintain your consult thread, send receipts or case links, respond to follow-up messages, provide status updates, and operate and improve the service.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your full card number. Stripe may process payment information according to its own privacy and security practices.

Email and SMS/Text Updates

If you provide your phone number and consent to call/text updates, we may send transactional text messages related to your paid consult. These may include the paid thread link, case status updates, reply notifications, or other messages directly related to your active diagnostic consult.

SMS consent is optional and is not required to purchase a consult. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your consult activity.

No Sale or Sharing of SMS Consent

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile number, SMS opt-in consent, or SMS-related information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Service Providers

We may use service providers to operate the service, including hosting, database, payment, email, and messaging providers. These providers are used only as needed to provide the service and related communications.

Uploaded Photos, Audio, and Files

Photos, audio notes, and other files you upload are used to understand and respond to your consult. Please avoid submitting unrelated personal information.

Data Retention

We retain consult information as needed to provide the service, maintain records, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and improve operations. You may request deletion of your consult information, subject to legal, accounting, or operational retention needs.

Your Choices

You may opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP. You may request help by replying HELP. You may also contact us using the email address below.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to: rodgersservicesllc@hotmail.com.

This policy applies to What's it Doing? consult services available at whatsitdoing.app.
