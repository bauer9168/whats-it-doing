What's it Doing?

Automotive Repair Consultation Service

Business and service context

What's it Doing? is an automotive diagnostic guidance service operated by Rodgers Services LLC / Rodgers Auto Repair in Rochester, Washington.

The service provides written automotive diagnostic guidance based on the information a customer submits, including symptoms, vehicle details, trouble codes, repair history, photos, audio notes, estimates, skill/knowledge level, and follow-up messages.

Not a car person? The goal is to translate the symptoms, codes, repair history, and shop information into plain language so customers can better understand what may be happening, what may be worth checking next, and which repair decisions may deserve more caution, testing, or confidence.

Not AI — real-world, experience-based feedback.

How the paid consult works

  1. The customer submits vehicle details, symptoms, concerns, and optional photos, files, or audio notes.
  2. The customer chooses a paid consult option and completes checkout.
  3. The customer receives a paid thread link by email, and by text message only if they choose optional text updates.
  4. The issue is worked through by written thread first. If needed, a phone call may be used.
  5. When completed, the thread contents and final notes form the written diagnostic review.

SMS program context

Text messages are transactional consult updates only. They may include a paid thread link, case status updates, reply notifications, or updates related to an active paid consult. SMS consent is optional and is not required to purchase a consult.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.
